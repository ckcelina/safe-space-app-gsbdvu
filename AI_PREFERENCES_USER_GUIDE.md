
# AI Style Preferences - User Guide

## What Are AI Style Preferences?

Safe Space now lets you customize how the AI communicates with you. Choose from 22 different tone styles and optionally enable psychology insights.

## How to Set Your Preferences

### During Onboarding (New Users)
1. Create your account
2. You'll see the "Choose Your AI Style" screen
3. Browse tones by category:
   - **Gentle & Supportive**: Warm, empathetic, comforting
   - **Balanced & Clear**: Mix of empathy and practicality
   - **Direct & Firm**: Straightforward, action-oriented
4. Toggle "Science & Resources Mode" if desired
5. Tap "Continue" or "Skip for now"

### In Settings (Anytime)
1. Go to Settings (bottom tab)
2. Find "AI Style Preferences" card
3. Tap "AI Tone" to see all 22 options
4. Toggle "Science & Resources Mode" on/off
5. Changes save automatically

## FAQs

**Q: Will changing my tone delete my conversations?**
A: No! Your conversations are preserved. Only future messages use the new tone.

**Q: Can I have different tones for different people?**
A: Not yet, but this is a great idea for a future update!

**Q: What if I don't like any tone?**
A: Balanced Blend is designed to work for most people. Give it a try!

**Q: Does Science mode make responses longer?**
A: Slightly, but only when it adds value. It won't overwhelm you.

**Q: Can I preview tones before choosing?**
A: Currently no, but you can change anytime. Try a few to find your favorite!
